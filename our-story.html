How We Met

His Perspective

My friends and I decided to go to Gallette's one Wednesday night in Tuscaloosa. Over the course of the next few hours I kept making eye contact with this pretty girl. I of course couldn't work up the courage to go talk to her. Fortunately for me, she came up and introduced herself right as the bar was closing and everyone was shuffling out. I almost let her go, but came to my senses and ran after her and asked for her number.

We started talking the next day and that was pretty much it. Our first date was at Desperado's Steakhouse and it was very apparent that we had a lot in common. I asked her to be my date for my fraternity's formal in New Orleans. On that trip I think we both realized we were meant for each other.
